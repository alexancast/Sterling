1. Position and Duties

The Employer hereby employs the Employee as a Private Investigator at Sterling & Co. Investigations. The Employee agrees to perform the duties and responsibilities assigned to them by the Employer, including but not limited to conducting private investigations, gathering evidence, interviewing witnesses, surveillance, and providing detailed reports of findings.

The Employee agrees to perform all assigned tasks with due diligence, confidentiality, and in accordance with the law.

2. Confidentiality

The Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality concerning all information and materials related to clients, cases, investigations, and any other sensitive business matters of the Employer. This obligation remains in effect even after the termination of employment. Any breach of confidentiality may result in immediate dismissal and legal action.

3. Code of Conduct

The Employee agrees to uphold the highest ethical standards while performing their duties and shall refrain from engaging in any illegal, unethical, or immoral activities, whether on or off duty. Any such activities will result in immediate termination.

The Employee shall also be responsible for maintaining a professional demeanor, including appropriate dress, conduct, and communication with clients and associates.

4. Non-Compete Clause

The Employee agrees that, for the duration of their employment and for a period of 1 year following the termination of employment, they shall not engage in or be associated with any competing private investigation firm within a 50-mile radius of Sterling & Co. Investigations.

5. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings, whether written or oral, relating to the employment of the Employee by the Employer.
